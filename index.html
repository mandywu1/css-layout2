Liu Wei, The Revolutionary Family Series (triptych), 1994. Estimate HK$30,000,000–40,000,000. Although The Revolutionary Family Series was recognised as Liu Wei’s signature early on, he only pursued it for a few years. The present triptych, which Liu Wei created on a special commission in 1994, represents the height of his early career. Its majestic composition is unseen elsewhere in his early oeuvre, and adding to its rarity, it comes with a unique hand-painted frame. Tanaka Atsuko, 90C, 1990. Estimate HK$1,800,000–2,800,000.

This mesmerising painting features jostling, pulsating circles that evoke the frenzied lights of Japan’s neon cityscape. An electrifying symphony celebrating the sublimity of life and interconnectivity, Tanaka’s omnipresent circles and lines are the culmination of her groundbreaking, career-long explorations in sound, performance, technology and mixed media.
